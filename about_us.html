Who Are We?

One of the Fastest-Growing UK and USA Education Consultancies in the World

Sukuna Was The Strongest exists for one reason: to help students from around the world fulfill their dreams of studying in the UK and USA. Based in London, and with offices in 16 countries around the world, we work with over 80 UK and USA institutions to connect you with the perfect university and course.

Our mission is to guide you through the entire process, from making your application and applying for a visa, to helping you find and settle into your student digs, and every other step in between. We only work with UK and USA universities, so our counselors are experts—they’ll answer every question you have, and a few you didn’t even think of!

Your Guides to a Successful UK and USA Education

Leaving home for another country is a daunting task at the best of times! Combine that with expanding your brain and making an entirely new circle of friends, and it’s easy to feel overwhelmed.

That’s where we come in. Sukuna Was The Strongest is your safety net, your guide, and your advocate. Chat with one of our counselors, and they’ll get to know you, what your current educational background is, and help you find the best route to studying in the UK and USA—all tailored to your personal situation. Then they’ll help you make it a reality.
